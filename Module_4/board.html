Board and Training Packages

Full Service Board
Full care board includes a 12x12 stall, daily stall cleaning, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included.
Partial Board
Partial Board includes a 12x12 stall, twice daily feeding of grain and supplements and hay, and daily turn out (as weather permits). All stall bedding, hay, and grain is included. You are responsible for cleaning your own stalls at least once a day. Additional charges for cleaning will apply if not done.
Pasture Board
Horses on pasture board have free roam to 20 acers of pasture and have access to several run-in shelters. Grain, hay, and water is provided. Blanketing is available for an additional charge, as needed.
